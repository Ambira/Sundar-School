About Us

Sundar School is dedicated to fostering excellence in education and was established with the vision of providing quality education. We aim to develop well-rounded individuals who are not only academically proficient but also socially responsible. We take pride in our inclusive approach, innovative teaching methods, and dedicated faculty, ensuring that every student receives personalized attention and support. We prepare our students for the challenges of the 21st century while staying rooted in cultural values.

Academics

Our academic programs are designed to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. We follow a well-structured curriculum tailored to meet the needs of diverse learners while adhering to national and international standards. Our dedicated faculty ensures that students are not just taught but inspired to learn.

  • Interactive and engaging classroom experiences
  • Integration of technology into learning
  • A focus on STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics)
  • Arts and humanities for a well-rounded education

Student Life

Sundar School offers a vibrant and enriching environment where students can thrive academically and personally. Our extracurricular programs are designed to nurture talents, build confidence, and encourage collaboration.

Sports and Athletics

Promoting physical health and teamwork through various sports programs.

Music, Dance, and Arts

Encouraging creativity and self-expression through artistic pursuits.

Community Service Projects

Fostering empathy and social responsibility among students.

Robotics and Coding Clubs

Building technical skills and nurturing innovation.

Cultural Celebrations

Celebrating diversity and fostering a sense of community.

About Sundar School

Sundar School aims to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where children can grow emotionally, socially, and academically. With our dedicated staff and innovative curriculum, we focus on holistic education that engages every child.

Principal's Voice

A message from our esteemed Principal

Dear Parents, Guardians, and Well-Wishers,

Welcome to Sundar School, where every child’s potential is nurtured, and dreams take flight. At Sundar School, we believe that education is more than just academics; it’s about shaping character, fostering creativity, and building lifelong learners equipped to thrive in a dynamic world.

Our vision is to create an inclusive and empowering environment that celebrates diversity and encourages each student to discover their unique strengths. Guided by our core values of integrity, compassion, and innovation, we are committed to providing a holistic education that blends tradition with modernity.

I invite you to join us in this journey of shaping the future—together. Let’s inspire young minds to lead, contribute, and create a brighter tomorrow.

Warm regards,

Shyam Sundar Chaudhary, PhD

Principal

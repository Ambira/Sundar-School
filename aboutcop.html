About Us

Sundar School is dedicated to fostering excellence in education and was established with the vision of providing quality education. Our aim is to develop well-rounded individuals who are academically proficient and socially responsible. We pride ourselves on an inclusive approach, innovative teaching methods, and a faculty committed to personalized attention and student success. We equip our students with the skills to excel in the 21st century, all while maintaining a strong foundation in cultural values.

Facilities

At Sundar School, we are committed to providing an environment that encourages learning and personal growth. Our state-of-the-art facilities cater to the diverse needs of our students, supporting academic excellence and extracurricular engagement.

Library

Our well-stocked library is a hub for knowledge, offering students access to a wide range of books, digital resources, and study spaces designed to foster a love for reading and independent learning.

Science Labs

Equipped with modern apparatus, our science labs encourage hands-on learning and experimentation, providing students with the opportunity to explore the fascinating world of physics, chemistry, and biology.

Sports Facilities

Our expansive sports facilities, including a multi-sport field, gymnasium, and swimming pool, promote physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition among students.

Art Studio

The art studio is a creative space for students to explore various forms of visual arts, encouraging self-expression, innovation, and appreciation for the arts.

Music Room

Our music room, filled with a variety of instruments, allows students to explore their musical talents, whether through individual lessons or group performances in choir and band.

Outdoor Spaces

Spacious outdoor areas, including playgrounds, gardens, and sports courts, provide students with opportunities to engage in physical activities and unwind in nature, enhancing both physical and mental well-being.
